Peer Review Process

All submissions to EasyJournal undergo a rigorous peer review process:

  1. Initial screening by editors for scope and basic quality
  2. Double-blind peer review by at least two subject matter experts
  3. Editorial decision based on reviewer recommendations
  4. Revisions and re-review as necessary
  5. Final decision and publication

Publication Ethics

EasyJournal adheres to the highest standards of publication ethics, including:

  • Original content free from plagiarism
  • Proper attribution and citation of sources
  • Disclosure of conflicts of interest
  • Ethical research methodologies
  • Transparency in reporting results
